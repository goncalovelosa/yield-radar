## Methodology

Each persona was asked to simulate browsing the landing page at [yieldradar.io](https://yieldradar.io) and answer:

1. **First impression** — what do you see in 3 seconds?
2. **Value proposition clarity** — do you understand what this is?
3. **Trust signals** — would you trust this with your money/data?
4. **Pricing perception** — does the pricing make sense for you?
5. **Missing information** — what questions do you have that aren't answered?
6. **Conversion friction** — what's stopping you from clicking "Open in Telegram"?
7. **Competitive positioning** — how does this compare to what you already use?
8. **Specific issues** — factual errors, broken links, outdated data, confusing copy
