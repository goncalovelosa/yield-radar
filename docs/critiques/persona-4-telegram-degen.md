# Persona 4: The Telegram Degen

**Name:** Diego (24, university student, São Paulo)
**Capital:** $15K rotating across memecoin and high-APY plays
**Experience:** 1 year DeFi, started with memecoins on Solana, now farms on Raydium, Jupiter, Orca
**Risk Tolerance:** High — comfortable losing 50%+ on a play, chases high APY
**Telegram:** Extremely heavy — 20+ crypto groups, Banana Gun daily, follows alpha callers
**Current spend:** Banana Gas fees (variable), no subscriptions

---

## Journey Simulation

### Hero Section
**Sees:** "18,632 yield pools on DeFiLlama. Most of them will lose you money."
**Thinks:** "Lol yeah. But I'm not most people — I find the good ones. Wait, actually I do lose money sometimes. What's this?"
**Verdict:** ✅ "Most of them will lose you money" actually resonates with Diego's experience. Attention-grabbing.

### The Problem Section
**Sees:** "Finding the good ones means spending hours on aggregators..."
**Thinks:** "True. I spend way too much time scrolling DeFiLlama and DexScreener. If this pushes the good stuff to my Telegram, I'm in."
**Verdict:** ✅ Problem statement hits home.

### How It Works
**Sees:** Fetch → Filter → Score → Alert
**Thinks:** "OK so it checks pools and sends me alerts. But does it catch the NEW high-APY pools fast? Like, if a new pool launches at 200% APY, how fast does this bot tell me? My alpha groups tell me in seconds."
**Verdict:** ⚠️ Speed isn't emphasized enough. Diego cares about being FIRST. "Updated every 5 minutes" is actually slow for a degen.

### Risk Scoring Section
**Sees:** 4 tiers (Safe/Watch/Speculative/Danger)
**Thinks:** "Honestly, I don't really care about the scoring. I want the highest APY pools. But the danger zone flagging — that's useful. If it tells me a pool is a honeypot, that saves me money."
**Verdict:** ⚠️ Risk scoring is less important to this persona than SPEED and YIELD. The page doesn't emphasize yield discovery.

### Preview / Mockups
**Sees:** Aerodrome 19.1%, Uniswap V4 17.9%, Raydium 47.1% (flagged)
**Thinks:** "These APYs are kinda mid. 19%? I've seen 200%+ on Solana. Where are the high-APY pools? And the Raydium one is flagged — but I've farmed that pair before and made money. Is the scoring too conservative?"
**Verdict:** ❌ Mockup APYs are too conservative for this persona. Diego would think "this bot only finds boring pools." The flagged Raydium example might feel overly cautious.

### Comparison Table
**Sees:** YieldRadar vs Alpha Telegram groups
**Thinks:** "Alpha groups = spam. True. But some of my groups have genuine alpha. Does this bot catch stuff my groups don't?"
**Verdict:** ⚠️ Doesn't answer the key question: "Can this bot find pools I wouldn't find through my existing channels?"

### Getting Started
**Sees:** 3 steps, "No wallet needed. No KYC. No sign-up."
**Thinks:** "Perfect. I'm in. Let me try it."
**Verdict:** ✅ Getting started is frictionless — exactly what Diego wants.

### Pricing
**Sees:** Free (3 alerts/day), Pro ($9.99/mo)
**Thinks:** "3 alerts per day?! I want alerts ALL DAY. $9.99/mo is fair. But do I need to pay in Telegram Stars? How does that work? I've never bought Stars before."
**Verdict:** ⚠️ 3 alerts/day is too restrictive to show value. Diego would hit that in 10 minutes and think "this is useless" before converting. ⚠️ No explanation of how Telegram Stars payment works.

### Chain Coverage
**Sees:** Ethereum, Base, Solana, Arbitrum, BSC, Polygon
**Thinks:** "Solana is there, good. But where's Hyperliquid? That's where the real yield is right now. And Pump.fun pools — does this track those?"
**Verdict:** ⚠️ Missing hot chains (Hyperliquid). Diego is on whatever chain has the best yield right now.

### Security Section
**Sees:** Non-custodial, GoPlus, Open source
**Thinks:** "Don't really care about this stuff. I just want the alpha. But non-custodial is good — I've been scammed by a custodial bot before."
**Verdict:** ⚠️ Security section is wasted space for this persona. Would rather see more yield-focused content.

### Moat Section
**Sees:** Historical data advantage, continuous scoring refinement
**Thinks:** "Boring. I don't care about historical data. I care about NOW."
**Verdict:** ❌ Moat section is irrelevant to Diego. This whole page is too "institutional" for a degen audience.

## Key Findings

### Critical Issues
1. **Free tier is too restrictive for the persona most likely to convert** — 3 alerts/day is useless to someone who wants yield discovery all day
2. **No speed emphasis** — "5 minutes" is slow for a degen. Should emphasize "first to know" or "before the crowd"
3. **Mockup APYs too conservative** — Diego wants to see high-APY examples (50%+), not 17-19%
4. **Missing hot chains** — Hyperliquid is the current yield hotspot, not listed

### Moderate Issues
5. **Page tone too institutional** — Moat section, methodology details, security deep-dives are wasted on degens
6. **No Telegram Stars payment explanation** — Diego has never bought Stars and needs guidance
7. **Risk scoring over-emphasized** — Diego cares more about yield discovery than risk management
8. **No "alpha" framing** — doesn't position itself as a tool for finding opportunities others miss

### Minor Issues
9. **No social/community signals** — Diego follows influencers, not product pages. No Twitter/X link.
10. **"Alpha Telegram groups = spam" is dismissive** — some groups are genuinely useful, and Diego knows which ones

### What Works Well
- Getting Started section (frictionless)
- Non-custodial (relevant after being scammed)
- Telegram-native (where Diego already lives)
- "No wallet, no KYC" (perfect for degen workflow)

## Diego's Unanswered Questions
- "How fast are alerts? If a new pool hits 200% APY, how long before I get notified?"
- "Can I get alerts for memecoin LP pairs or only 'serious' pools?"
- "How do Telegram Stars work? Do I need to buy them in-app?"
- "Why only 3 alerts per day? Can't I just get all the high-APY pools?"
- "Where's Hyperliquid? That's where the real yield is."
- "Does this track Pump.fun or only DEX pools?"
- "Can I set a minimum APY filter? Like 'only show me pools above 50%'?"

## Recommendation
The Free tier's 3 alerts/day is the biggest conversion blocker for this persona. Consider: 5 alerts/day for free, or a 7-day free trial of Pro with unlimited alerts. Emphasize speed in the hero ("Be the first to know"). Add high-APY examples to mockups. Add Hyperliquid to chains ASAP. Link to Twitter/X for social proof.
